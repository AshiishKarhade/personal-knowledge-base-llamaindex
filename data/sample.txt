## About LlamaIndex

LlamaIndex is a data framework for building LLM applications. It provides tools for:
- Ingesting data from various sources
- Creating indexes for efficient retrieval
- Querying documents with natural language

## RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)

RAG combines retrieval of relevant documents with generative AI to provide accurate, context-aware responses.
